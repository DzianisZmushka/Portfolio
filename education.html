Education

Kalinkovichi gymnasium

I was born in the Belarusian city of Kalinkovichi. During the period of study at the Kalinkovichi gymnasium, I won the Gomel regional school olympiad in German language eight times and once in mathematics.

Belarusian State Economic University

In 2019, I graduated from the Belarusian State Economic University, namely the branch of the world economy (management of foreign economic activity) of the Faculty of International Economic Relations. During my studies at the university, I won the student olympiad in higher mathematics and German language. In addition, there I studied such subjects as mathematical programming, probability theory, mathematical statistics, econometrics, computer information technologies, macroeconomics, microeconomics, international investment, accounting, credit and settlement operations, management of foreign economic activity, world economy, international integration , international management, marketing and other economic and mathematical subjects.
